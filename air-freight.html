Air Freight Forwarding Services

Based in Hargeisa (HGA), Airsell Cargo provides seamless air connectivity to global hubs including Dubai (DXB), Addis Ababa (ADD), and worldwide destinations via our strategic partnership with Ethiopian Airlines (ET).

Cargo Acceptance Checklist

  • AWB Accuracy: Precise Air Waybill preparation (IATA Reso 600a).
  • Ready for Carriage: Proper packaging, marking, and labeling.
  • Security: Full compliance with HGA Airport security screening.
  • Dangerous Goods: Specialized handling for regulated items.

Volumetric Weight Calculation

As per international standards, freight charges are based on the greater of the actual weight or volumetric weight.
